About Us

Welcome to Lu Lab @ Tsinghua University
-- a bioinformatics group

We are group of people dedicated to the research of bioinformatics, genomics and systems biology. We are interested in understanding how information is encoded in the structured DNA and RNA sequences, and how they regulate one another in a biological system. Ultimately, it will help us to understand and cure human diseases.

more about us »

Research Interests

The research interest of our lab is to predict and characterize novel noncoding RNA's structure and function, using bioinformatics and genomics methods. Meanwhile, we also develop computational methods (e.g. machine learning methods) to integrate various high-throughput data.
